Interstice

Interstice is a digital journal dedicated to exploring critical social issues, amplifying marginalized voices, and fostering inclusive dialogue. we aim to challenge systemic inequalities and inspire action through thoughtful writing on capitalism, colonial legacies, environmental degradation, culture, technology, and society. we invite all individuals concerned with justice and systemic change, interstice seeks to create a vibrant space for reflection, discussion, and advocacy, bridging ideas and action for a more equitable shared existence.
