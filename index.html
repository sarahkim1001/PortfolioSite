Sarah Seiyuen Kim is an interdisciplinary designer and multimedia artist shaping the aesthetics of human-machine interaction. With multifaceted approach, she creates transformative sensory experiences that resonate emotionally and physiologically.

Her work leverages her academic background in integrated design, digital media, and cultural studies to examine and redefine human interactions and identities. By engaging critical research, emerging technologies, and natural observation, Sarah crafts immersive narratives that invite reflection on the complexities of contemporary existence.

She has crafted multimedia projects that center on empowering individuals, deepening relationships, and shifting social fabrics, blending examinations of the human psyche within social, environmental, and conceptual ecologies. Her ideas have taken shape in various forms, utilizing her comprehensive background in multimedia integration, digital composition, interactive audio in MAX/MSP, creative coding, projection design and mapping, film development and photography, graphic design, web development, electronics, and physical fabrication.

Among other things, Sarah is an impressionist artist, experimental and classically trained cellist, writer, composer, and learner of new languages. She is inspired by phantasmagoria, natural patterns, and vanishing points. She is curious about semantics, cybernetics, and is endlessly enchanted by the mysteries of being~
